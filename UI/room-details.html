About This Room

Immerse yourself in luxury with our Deluxe Ocean Suite, featuring breathtaking panoramic views of the ocean. This spacious 45m² suite is elegantly designed with modern amenities and classic comfort. Wake up to the sound of waves and enjoy your morning coffee on the private balcony while watching the sunrise over the water.

The suite includes a plush king-size bed with premium linens, a comfortable seating area, and a marble bathroom with rain shower and luxury toiletries. Whether you're here for business or leisure, this room provides the perfect sanctuary for your stay.
